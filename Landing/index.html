India Mind League is envisioned as a new-age championship aligned with today's AI-driven world.

While AI can process knowledge, it cannot feel or make value-based decisions.

With the belief that education must nurture both intellect and values, this initiative focuses on developing Emotional Intelligence (EQ) and Spiritual Intelligence (SQ) in students.
